Stress, Trauma, and Resilience Centre

Carleton University

FOCUS

The focus of the Stress, Trauma, and Resilience Centre, as the name implies, is to identify the factors that favour the development of stress-related pathology, and concurrently determine the processes that promote resilience

INTERVENTION

In this regard, many stress-related pathologies are preventable illnesses, and thus considerable efforts are being made to identify interventions that limit their development.

OUTREACH

Moreover, considerable attention is devoted to community outreach, translating our research to appropriate treatment venues, as well as to inform policy that is derived from our research

MEMBERS

As such, our members are committed to knowledge translation and knowledge mobilization: we partner with many community-based organizations and individuals with lived experience as knowledge users.
